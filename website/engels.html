History and what you can do:

In 2008 Hasan had a garagebox with 10 arcade machines, the garagebox soon became too small en they moved to a warehouse. Soon it became clear that the warehouse wasn't heated and in te middle of winter everything had to be moved. Hasan rented a location with 4 friends and soon more people became interested in the machines and the location became too small again. In 2016 Hasan came in contact with Jan kragt, citymarketeer of Zoetermeer, he showed interest in the collection aswell. Jan came with the idea to make a museum with the collection en to establish a foundation for it. The city council was enthusiastic about the idea and decided to financially support the museum. In 2017 the museum got a fixed location and with the help of some volunteers, on december first 2017 the museum was ready for visitors. The museums collection is still growing and nowadays you can play all sorts of games like: Mariokart, Sonic Blast Heroes, Just Dance, Wii Sports, etc. All games in the Gamemuseum are free to play and they're all allowed to be played. You can also play with friends on our multiplayer games, you can sit next to eachother and play together in multiplayer so you can share your fun with friends and family!
