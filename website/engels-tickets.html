Information

When you book a ticket for our museum, you will get acces for 2 hours and you are allowed to play all games the museum offers. All arcademachines are 'free to play', so you won't need extra coins!

You can buy the tickets at the register of the museum. The buying of tickets works with timeslots, so you aren't buying a ticket for the whole day, but a ticket for a timeslot. During one of those timeslots, you will be able to walk in and out of the museum, you will get a stamp on your hand which gives you acces to the museum. So you are able to get food and drinks inbetween games!
