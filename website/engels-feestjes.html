Kid parties

The rate for a kids party is 13,50 euros a person including supervisors.

Tickets for a kids party are valid for a certain timeslot on the day and cannot be exchanged for a different timeslot.

The minimum number of people for a kids party is 6 including supervisors, the maximum is 10. If you want more than 10 people present you will need to contact us.

You will get a festively laid table where the whole group can eten and put their stuff. In connection with hygiene you are not allowed to bring your own foods, a cake is allowed.

Company outings

We will provide you with a fully catered lunch or diner, we can also serve you bitter garnish when you want to take a small break from gaming.

In addition to a lunch or diner, you will also get a tour of the museum. During that tour you will be led by different time-rooms and you will get a peak behind the scenes. Visit our technical serves and discover how we take care of our arcade machines.

Next to all of that you can organize a tournament, like a mariokart race or who gets the higest highscore in sonic blast heroes!
